Who says comic books are for children?

No comic books aren't exclusively for children. This article from 2017 says 43% of comic book buyers are over the age of 29. I started reading comic books as soon as I could read. As a Brazilian child my number one comic book is no surprise and I still enjoy the nostalgic feelings I got from time to time when I manage to buy one issue. Here are my top 3 comic books of my childhood:

  1. Turma da Mônica
  2. O Menino Maluquinho
  3. O Homem-Aranha
